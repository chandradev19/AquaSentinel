# AquaShield AI - User Manual

## Overview
AquaShield AI provides dedicated workflows for **Citizens** (the general public) and **Field Workers** (health inspectors and nurses).

## Citizen Workflow
The Citizen Portal empowers individuals to directly report health issues, bypassing bureaucratic delays.

- **Login:** `citizen1@gmail.com` / `citizen123`
- **Dashboard:** Displays real-time notifications, local water safety indices, and government advisories.
- **Submit Report:**
  - Navigate to the `Submit Report` page from the sidebar.
  - Select symptoms (e.g., Fever, Diarrhea, Vomiting).
  - Specify the severity and duration.
  - Submit. The AI will instantly geolocate the report and add it to the National Risk Matrix.
- **My Reports:** View the status of past submissions (Pending, Verified, Escalated).

## Field Worker Workflow
Field Workers act as the boots-on-the-ground agents to verify AI predictions and citizen reports.

- **Login:** `worker1@aquashield.gov` / `worker123`
- **Dashboard:** Displays tasks assigned specifically to the worker's jurisdiction.
- **Field Surveys:**
  - View a list of unverified Citizen Reports.
  - Visit the household to confirm the illness.
  - Mark the report as `VERIFIED` or `FALSE ALARM`.
- **Water Testing:**
  - Submit physical water test parameters (pH, Turbidity, Contaminant levels).
  - If the test indicates high contamination, the AI engine will automatically trigger a localized alert for all citizens in that village.
