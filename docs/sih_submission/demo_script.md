# AquaShield AI - 5-Minute Judge Demonstration Script

**Objective:** Showcase the end-to-end functionality of AquaShield AI, emphasizing real-time intelligence, the digital twin, and predictive capabilities.

---

### Minute 0:00 - 0:45 | The Problem & National Command Center
* **Action:** Start on the Admin Login page. Log in as `admin@aquashield.gov`.
* **Script:**
    * *"Welcome to AquaShield AI. Our platform solves the critical delay in identifying and mitigating waterborne disease outbreaks in rural India."*
    * *"We are starting at the National Health Command War Room. This is our central dashboard."*
* **Action:** Highlight the National Health Index and Live India Map.
    * *"Here we see our dynamic National Health Index. On the right is a live geospatial risk map showing our monitored villages."*
    * *"Notice the 'Critical High Risk' panel on the left. The AI has already flagged several locations based on telemetry."*

### Minute 0:45 - 1:30 | The Citizen Workflow
* **Action:** Open a new incognito window (or switch browsers). Navigate to Citizen Login. Log in as a citizen.
* **Script:**
    * *"How does data enter our system? We bypass bureaucratic delays by empowering citizens directly."*
* **Action:** Navigate to "Submit Report".
    * *"A citizen in a vulnerable district reports severe diarrhea and vomiting."*
    * *(Fill out the symptom report form and submit)*.
    * *"As soon as this is logged, it feeds directly into our intelligence core."*

### Minute 1:30 - 2:30 | Worker Verification & Digital Twin
* **Action:** Switch back to the Admin window. Navigate to "Villages" in the sidebar and click on the affected village to open the **Village Digital Twin**.
* **Script:**
    * *"Let's look at the specific village. This is the Village Digital Twin—a live cybernetic replica."*
* **Action:** Point out the recent Citizen Report in the feed, and the assigned Health Worker.
    * *"We instantly see the symptom report in the timeline. To prevent false alarms, a local field worker is automatically dispatched to verify the claim and conduct a water quality test."*
* **Action:** Click "Export Report PDF".
    * *"With one click, we can generate a comprehensive PDF report of this village for offline authorities."*

### Minute 2:30 - 3:30 | Outbreak Simulator & AI Prediction
* **Action:** Navigate to the "AI Command Center" -> "Outbreak Simulator".
* **Script:**
    * *"Now for the most powerful feature: The Outbreak Simulator."*
* **Action:** Adjust the "Water Contamination" and "Rainfall" sliders upwards.
    * *"Our backend uses a Weka RandomForest Machine Learning model. Let's ask the AI: 'What if monsoon rains increase turbidity and contamination by 40%?'"*
* **Action:** Click "Run AI Simulation".
    * *"The system instantly calculates the Epidemic Risk. It highlights a massive spike in potential Cholera outbreaks based on historical heuristics."*

### Minute 3:30 - 4:30 | Emergency Alerts & Mitigation
* **Action:** Navigate to the "Alerts" tab.
* **Script:**
    * *"Because the risk crossed critical thresholds, the Risk Engine has auto-generated a 'CRITICAL' Emergency Broadcast."*
* **Action:** Show the active alert.
    * *"This alert is immediately pushed to the mobile devices of all citizens and health officers in the affected district, instructing them to boil water and dispatching emergency chlorination supplies."*

### Minute 4:30 - 5:00 | Conclusion & Analytics
* **Action:** Navigate to "Analytics".
* **Script:**
    * *"Finally, all this data aggregates in our Analytics suite, allowing the Ministry of Health to track disease distribution over time."*
    * *"AquaShield AI changes outbreak management from a slow, reactive process into a real-time, predictive shield."*
    * *"Thank you. We are ready for questions."*
